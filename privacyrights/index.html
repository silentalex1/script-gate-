What is PrivacyRights?

PrivacyRights is an innovative Chrome extension designed to safeguard your online privacy and enhance your browsing experience. It functions as a comprehensive Chrome management tool that keeps your browser secure and fast.

With advanced detection capabilities, PrivacyRights identifies potentially harmful Chrome extensions that could compromise your security or slow down your browser. Additionally, the extension comes with a built-in adblocker that eliminates intrusive ads across the web.
