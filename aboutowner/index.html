Who I Am

Hey there! I'm a 16 year old high school student who loves building helpful browser extensions and coding. I'm always working to improve my skills as a developer to create tools that make using the web better for everyone, I also know c#, c++, lua, js, and python.

I balance my school work with several coding projects at once. While I'm still learning, I put in the effort to make sure everything I create works well and solves real problems.
