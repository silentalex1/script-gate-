What is StreamShift?

StreamShift looks like a simple productivity tool on the surface. It helps you organize tasks, manage time, and boost your workflow.

But there's more - StreamShift smartly reroutes your internet traffic in the background, helping you access content without dealing with network blocks. All while looking like a normal work app on your screen.

How It Works

Using StreamShift is simple:

  • Install the StreamShift app on your computer
  • Open it and see a normal productivity dashboard
  • Use the app normally for task tracking
  • In the background, it routes your traffic around blocks
  • Access any website without seeing block messages
  • Switch to "Focus Mode" if someone walks by your desk
